About FlipZone


FlipZone is India's fastest-growing internet commerce company aiming to make eCommerce accessible to everyone. FlipZone's is to enable 100 million small businesses in India, including individual entrepreneurs, to succeed online. FlipZone is democratizing internet commerce by bringing a range of products and new customers online. What started, six years ago, as a reseller-focused platform enabling millions to sell online, has now emerged as a single ecosystem connecting sellers to consumers and entrepreneurs.
